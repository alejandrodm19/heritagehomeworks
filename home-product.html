Annuvalotto is an architectural statement of refined French country design set within one of Utah's most coveted communities. The home's steeply pitched rooflines, natural stone cladding, and arched entryway create a first impression that is both timeless and breathtaking.

Inside, the open-concept great room flows seamlessly to a chef's kitchen outfitted with professional-grade Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances. Hand-selected Calacatta marble, custom cabinetry, and brushed brass hardware define every space with an understated luxury.

The primary suite, positioned on the main floor for single-level convenience, features a spa-inspired bath with heated floors, a soaking tub, and a walk-in closet designed by professional organizers. Four additional bedrooms — each with en-suite access — accommodate family and guests with equal elegance.
