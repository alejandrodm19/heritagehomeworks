Perched on one of the most desirable view lots in the neighborhood, this stunning custom home blends luxury design, thoughtful functionality, and incredible indoor-outdoor living. Offering 5,906 finished square feet and 6,822 total square feet, the home features 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms, a spacious main-floor primary suite, and expansive living areas designed for both everyday comfort and entertaining.

The chef’s kitchen is equipped with a 48” professional range, 60” built-in refrigerator, walk-in pantry, and a full service kitchen, complemented by custom cabinetry and built-ins throughout the home.

Vaulted ceilings and a dramatic 15-foot bifold sliding glass door open the main living area to a large view deck, creating seamless indoor-outdoor flow while capturing some of the best views in the neighborhood. Additional highlights include three fireplaces, three private decks, and a 10’ ceiling walk-out basement with space planned for a future home theater or golf simulator.

Situated on a 0.48 acre lot, the property features RV parking and a rare 12-foot-tall garage bay ideal for boats, trailers, or recreational vehicles. The home also offers a massive 4,000 sq ft fully graded backyard ready for your dream outdoor space, with additional land beyond the graded area providing even more opportunity for future landscaping, outdoor amenities, or expansion.
